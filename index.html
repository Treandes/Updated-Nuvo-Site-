About US

Gritty but beautiful. We teach for those who need it most and create for everyone.
Developing Amazing Things with Passion since 2014.

Shockingly enough, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics American young adults between the ages of 16 & 24 are twice as likely to be unemployed compared to other age groups. One of the prime reasons for this drastic unemployment drought among teens is the mandated wage hikes that policymakers have forced on small business. This hike inevitably makes people with little to no experience less susceptible to attaining the job that requires three to four years of experience.
Despite these results, unemployment statistics in fast growing industries like technology are depleting, creating a high demand for employees with experience. This is where we come in.
Nuvonic is a company with a dual-sided approach. On one side, we help students achieve the experience needed to get into the ever developing tech industry. We do this by having tailored instructors, whose passion is to teach web and application development deploying in their minds that it is possible to be something other then a famous basketball player or rapper.
Our second approach is a bit more business oriented. We work with clients creating web and applications focused products merging both creative intuition and business acquisition. We do this by having a creative and ingenious team whose main focus is to create a product that both the client and the end user would enjoy. If you would like more information about our company, feel free to email us.
